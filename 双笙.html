Shuangsheng

Shuangsheng, born Chen Yuanxi on May 13, 2000 in Kaizhou District, Chongqing, is an ancient Chinese female singer. Click this picture and you can enjoy the music:

Shuangsheng not only has a unique voice, but also has a slightly hoarse unique voice, and has a high recognition, her voice is described as "vicissitudes and tender".

Shuangsheng songs seem to have a special spiritual, special texture at first listen, then listen to the heartstrings, the more listen to the more people addicted.(Comprehensive review of Huanqiu, Eastday.com, Tencent Entertainment, Gaoling Economic Network, People's Daily Online)


Her sweet, ethereal voice is natural to the song when she sings the episode of the TV series "Chen Xi Yuan" called "Bird Language Forest". In singing the song "Yin and Yang Formula", emotional, long aftertaste; In the song "A Place to Live", she presented the story of Xiao Hei from wandering alone to meeting her master in a new form

Performance Experience:

  • In April 2015, Shuangsheng released a cover version of the ancient music "Old Dream", which officially entered the public eye. In the same year, the original single "Tea Picking Ji" was released.


  • In April 2016, the song "Child" was officially launched; May 13, released the album "Sheng Sheng" : in the same year, cover song "ninety-nine eighty-one".

  • In March 2017, performed the promotional song "A Lifelong Mistake" for the youth version of the TV series "A Dream of Red Mansions"; In April, she sang the medley song "Qian Yin Liu Nian"; In the same month, fan song "No Biography - Oriental Invincibility"; Sing the song "Linglong Ask".

  • On January 13, 2018, it released the original single "Girl's Demon Master"; In the same month, she sang City Surprise Night with GAI. January 25, singing ma Bu Ballad. On March 24, she released her single "Sannan". On August 24, the song yin-Yang Jizhi was officially released. On November 3, she won the Award of Most Popular Female singer of Chinese Style Musician. December 5, released the personal original single "Xie Fangfei".

  • In 2019, he released singles One-way Arrow, Birds' Language Forest, Goodbye Monkey King, And Legend like This, and his solo album Thorns, etc. On April 20, won the "Singer Award" in the "Young China" National style Music Festival and "young people's favorite National style music" award ceremony.

  • In January 2020, she won the title of "best female musician of the year" in the "5sing2019 annual gala". In the same year, he released singles such as Heat Island Disease, Trouble Don't Find Me, Qian Xi and Handwritten Scroll, I Love You, At Last, Tao Yao, Moonlight Night and Followers of Aristotle.
