Friends

Friends Season 6:

Ross and Rachel decided to go to court to cancel their marriage after they found out they were drunk, but Ross was worried that his divorce was too frequent, so they didn't cancel it and lied to Rachel. Rachel found out and argued, which eventually led to divorce. Ross got a job teaching paleontology at New York University. Chandler moved to Monica's house and decided not to get married for the time being, but to live together first. Rachel lives with Phoebe, but after a fire, she moves to Monica's house. Phoebe thought she had caused the fire and settled down with Joey, who was in poor condition. Later, it was found that the fire was originally caused by Rachel's hair straightener, so they changed their residence. The change of house caused a series of ironic stories, and in the end, Chandler finally decided to propose to Monica.



Friends Season 7:

Phoebe and Rachel's apartment has been repaired. Phoebe moves back to the apartment. Rachel continues to live with Joey. Joey got a role in the crew of days of our lives. Just the night before Chandler and Monica's wedding, Chandler suddenly flinched, ran away and hid. After he left, Phoebe found a pregnancy test stick showing pregnancy in the bathroom and concluded that Monica was pregnant. Phoebe and Ross find Chandler hiding in the office and persuade him to come back. When he comes back, he hears Phoebe whispering with Rachel - Monica is pregnant! Chandler and Monica finally got married, but Monica said she wasn't pregnant.


Friends Season 8:

In the public speculation, Rachel confirmed that she was indeed pregnant, and the child's father was Ross. It happened a month before Chandler and Monica's wedding. Rachel and Joey continue to live together, but Joey suddenly has a different feeling for Rachel. When Ross felt that he had lost the feeling of being a father, Joey had to suggest that Rachel move to Ross. Later, Joey expressed her feelings for Rachel, but Rachel declined. Rachel gave birth to a daughter named Emma, although the name Monica had planned to use for her children since childhood.


Friends Season 9:

Ross and Rachel live together with their daughter, but they are not in love. Finally, Rachel takes her daughter back to Joey and gradually feels about Joey. Chandler and Monica wanted a baby, but they always failed. After examination, they found that they both had physical problems, so they decided to adopt one. Phoebe dates Mike, but she always has to face the problem that Mike won't get married. Joey took Charlie to Barbados for a holiday with Ross. This trip changed the feelings of Ross, Rachel and Joey.


Friends Season 10:

Joey and Rachel soon found that they didn't adapt to each other and decided to just be friends. Mike overcomes his own obstacles. Mike finally proposes to Phoebe, and the two get married. Chandler and Monica's surrogate mother Erica gave birth to twins. Rachel decided to work in Paris through Mark's introduction. Her friends held a farewell party for her. In addition to rose, she took turns to say goodbye to her friends. At this time, Ross realized that he still loved Rachel. They made up their old friendship, and Rachel gave up her job in Paris. After ten years of ups and downs, everyone left their apartment and left their own keys.
